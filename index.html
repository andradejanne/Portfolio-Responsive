About Me

Coding was a topic I have been curious about. I finally took a leap of faith by taking Skillcrush courses. I wanted to dive in and become a Web Developer intertwined with an eye for Web Design.

During my free time, I love playing tennis, spending time with my boyfriend and my pug, and enjoying a good boba tea!

My goal to you is to design & code beautiful websites through Front End Development & Visual Design.

Construct the bare bones of your desired site by developing a wireframe then code with HTML5.

Then convert wireframes with CSS and Adobe Photoshop to match your personality and business.

Last, we test and check for any tweaks to launch your website!
